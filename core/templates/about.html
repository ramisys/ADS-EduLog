About EduLog

Empowering educational institutions with cutting-edge attendance and performance monitoring solutions to enhance student success and streamline administrative workflows.

Our Mission

To revolutionize student monitoring by providing intuitive, data-driven tools that enable educators to identify trends, address challenges proactively, and celebrate student achievements. We believe every student deserves personalized attention and support to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted education technology partner, transforming how schools monitor, analyze, and improve student outcomes. We envision a future where data-informed decisions lead to measurable improvements in student engagement, attendance, and academic excellence.

Our Story

As IT students, we embarked on this journey to create EduLog as our final requirement for Advanced Database Systems (ADS) under the guidance of our wonderful instructor, Ma'am Sarah Jane Cabral. This project represents not just an academic requirement, but our passion for applying database technologies to solve real-world educational challenges.

What started as a final project requirement has evolved into a comprehensive student attendance and performance monitoring system. Through this journey, we've learned to integrate database design, web development, and user experience principles to create a platform that can truly make a difference in how schools track and manage student data.

We are grateful to Ma'am Sarah Jane Cabral for her guidance, support, and encouragement throughout this project. Her dedication to teaching and mentoring has inspired us to push beyond our limits and create something we're truly proud of. This project has been an incredible learning experience, combining our technical skills with practical application in the field of education technology.

EduLog is a testament to what IT students can achieve when passion meets purpose, and when great instructors like Ma'am Sarah Jane Cabral guide us along the way.
