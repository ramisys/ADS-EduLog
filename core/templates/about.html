About EduLog

Empowering educational institutions with cutting-edge attendance and performance monitoring solutions to enhance student success and streamline administrative workflows.

Our Mission

To revolutionize student monitoring by providing intuitive, data-driven tools that enable educators to identify trends, address challenges proactively, and celebrate student achievements. We believe every student deserves personalized attention and support to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted education technology partner, transforming how schools monitor, analyze, and improve student outcomes. We envision a future where data-informed decisions lead to measurable improvements in student engagement, attendance, and academic excellence.

Our Story

EduLog was founded in 2020 by a team of educators and technologists who witnessed firsthand the challenges schools faced in tracking student attendance and performance efficiently.

What started as a simple attendance tracking tool has evolved into a comprehensive platform used by over 500 schools worldwide, serving more than 100,000 students and their families.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our capabilities, working closely with educators to ensure our platform meets the evolving needs of modern education.
